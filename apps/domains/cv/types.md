# CV Types and Layout Characteristics

## German Lebenslauf (classic)
- Header: name + contact at top, left-aligned.
- Often single-column, section headers in German (e.g., "Persönliche Daten", "Berufserfahrung").
- Dates commonly in `MM/YYYY` or `MM.YYYY` on the left, entries on the right.
- Photo sometimes top-right.

## Europass
- Distinct blue header, Europass branding.
- Strong sectioning with labels like "Work Experience", "Education and Training", "Personal Information".
- Frequently two-column layout with left label column and right content column.

## Modern résumé
- Minimalist, often two columns.
- Section headers in English (e.g., "Experience", "Education", "Skills").
- Emphasis on keywords and bullet lists.
- Icons for contact info (email/phone/location).

## Academic CV
- Longer, multi-page.
- Sections: publications, research, teaching, grants.
- Dense text, structured lists, often with years aligned to the right.
